About Us

A school that will help you achive your dreams and also help your relationship with God

God Inspired School was a revelation given to me by Almighty God to help people all around the world to achieve their dreams and to also have a great relationship with God. This school will be a free school to help those who can't afford to pay for their eduction. It is for children from the age of 2years to any age they will use to complete. It is from kindergarten to the university. There will be a free accomodation for students who are from very far places and there will be jobs opportunities for the students after completion of the school.
